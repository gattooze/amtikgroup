How We Work

From Resin to Shipment

Every order follows a documented, quality-gated process — from raw material receipt to final dispatch.

01
Raw Material Incoming Inspection

All resins — PP, HDPE, LDPE, and specialty grades — are received with Material Test Reports and batch-inspected for melt flow index, colour match, and contamination before admission to store.

02
Mould Setup & Process Parameter Setting

Machine setters configure melt temperature, injection pressure, cycle time, and cooling parameters per the documented process sheet for each mould. First-article samples are inspected before production begins.

03
In-Process Quality Sampling

AQL-based sampling at defined intervals during every production run. Dimensional checks, visual inspection, and functional tests are documented in the batch production record.

04
Finishing & Printing

Where applicable — hot-foil stamping, pad printing, or liner insertion carried out on the finished parts before packing.

05
Final Inspection & Packing

Outgoing QC: batch sample re-inspection, torque testing, and quantity count before parts are packed into bags, cartons, and pallets to client specification.

06
Dispatch & Documentation

Delivery challans, COA (Certificate of Analysis), and batch traceability records accompany every shipment. Delivery managed by road freight or client-nominated logistics.
