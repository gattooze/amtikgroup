Our Story

A Legacy of Precision & Quality

Established in 1990, Amtik Group specialises in manufacturing and exporting precision injection and blow moulded products for global industries. With over three decades of experience, we produce caps and closures — including flip top, FEZ, stand-up, and pharmaceutical varieties — used across pharmaceutical, cosmetic, and FMCG sectors.

We serve multinational companies across India and globally, combining new-age manufacturing technology with the reliability that only decades of experience can provide.
