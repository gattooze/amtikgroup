Our Story

From One Machine to a Full Manufacturing Ecosystem

Amtik Group was established in 1990 by a group of engineering professionals who believed that India's fast-growing consumer goods industry deserved a domestic manufacturer capable of matching international quality standards in plastic packaging components.

Starting with a single injection moulding machine in Mumbai, we grew steadily — investing in tooling capability, expanding our machine fleet, earning our ISO certification, and building long-term relationships with clients who wanted not just a supplier, but a reliable partner.

Today, from our purpose-built facility in Vasai East, Palghar, we operate 32+ moulding machines across injection and blow moulding technologies — producing over 120 product variants for India's most demanding brand owners.

Quality Credentials

ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Our quality management system is independently audited and certified to ISO 9001:2015 — the international benchmark for manufacturing quality. This isn't a plaque on the wall; it's the backbone of how we operate.

  • Document-controlled production processes and SOPs
  • Incoming raw material inspection with material test reports
  • In-process AQL sampling at defined intervals
  • Dimensional inspection with calibrated gauges
  • Functional testing — torque, drop, pressure, leak-proof
  • Non-conformance tracking and corrective action system
  • Annual management review and internal audit cycle
