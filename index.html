About Amtik Group

30 Years of Manufacturing Excellence

Incepted in 1990, Amtik Group specialises in manufacturing and exporting precision injection and blow moulded products — from laminated tube caps to specialised closures for pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, edible oils, and more.

Our fully automated facility in Mumbai runs 37 injection moulding machines and 3 blow moulding machines, producing 50M+ units every month with zero compromise on quality.

  • Complete solutions from concept to finished product
  • ISO 9001 certified quality management
  • In-house tooling — no outsourcing, faster turnarounds
  • Custom design and rapid prototyping capabilities
