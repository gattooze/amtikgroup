Who We Are

Forty Years of Precision, Zero Compromise

Founded in 1990 in Mumbai, Amtik Group has grown into one of India's most trusted manufacturers of plastic caps and closures. We combine state-of-the-art injection and blow moulding technology with deep domain expertise to deliver packaging components that protect and perform.

From our ISO 9001:2015 certified facility in Vasai, we serve leading brands across FMCG, pharmaceuticals, personal care, cosmetics, and industrial sectors — with exacting quality standards and on-time delivery at scale.

  • ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management system
  • 30+ injection & blow moulding machines in-house
  • Custom tooling & mould development capability
  • Serving 50+ major brands across India
