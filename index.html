About Amtik Group

New Age Manufacturing with Decades of Expertise

Incepted in 1990, Amtik Group specialises in manufacturing and exporting a comprehensive range of precision injection and blow moulded products — from laminated tube caps to specialised closures for pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, edible oils, and more.

  • Complete solutions — concept to final product
  • State-of-the-art injection & blow moulding facility
  • ISO certified quality management systems
  • Custom design and rapid prototyping capabilities
